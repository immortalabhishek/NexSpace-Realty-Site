FAQ

Your questions, answered.

What types of commercial properties do you handle?

We specialize in office spaces, retail units, warehouses, and industrial properties across 8 major Indian cities including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Pune.

Do you handle both leasing and sales?

Yes. We support commercial leasing, outright purchases, and sale-leaseback arrangements with full transaction support from search to handover.

What is the typical deal timeline?

Leasing typically takes 2-4 weeks. Outright purchases take 45-90 days depending on due diligence, legal verification, and registration.

Are your agents RERA registered?

Yes. All NexSpace Realty agents are RERA registered and undergo regular commercial real estate training to ensure expert guidance at every step.
